The Process

From Stone to Heirloom

01
Stone Curation

Each stone is personally selected from ethical mines. Only the top 1% of specimens are accepted into our atelier.

02
Design & Sketch

Our master designers draft each piece by hand before translating to 3D parametric models reviewed for structural integrity.

03
Master Craft

Skilled goldsmiths spend 40–80 hours per piece, hand-finishing every surface to a flawless, mirror-like standard.

04
Certified & Yours

Every piece ships with a full GIA-standard certificate, care kit, and a custom engraving of your choosing.
